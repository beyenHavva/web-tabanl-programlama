The Prayer Center Of Orland Park

Overview

“He who builds a prayer site (Masjid) for God, God will build a house for him in Paradise.”
Prophet Muhammad (Peace be upon him) 


The Prayer Center of Orland Park was established to worship the one and only God, the God of Abraham, Moses, Jesus, and the Last Messenger, Muhammad. Peace be upon all of them.
The Prayer Center of Orland Park has been organized as an Illinois non-for-profit corporation under the name THE PRAYER CENTER. The Prayer Center has obtained designation from the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) as a charitable, religious, and/or education organization under Section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code.
